About

Background, technology stack, and current focus.

Background

I am a backend developer focused on building reliable, production-oriented systems with Python. My work centers on API-driven architectures, asynchronous services, and AI-powered backend systems.

I primarily develop backend services using FastAPI and Python, emphasizing modular architecture, database design, and scalable service patterns. My projects typically integrate modern AI tooling such as LLM orchestration frameworks, vector databases, and automated workflows.

My development approach prioritizes:

  • clear system architecture
  • maintainable codebases
  • observable and testable services
  • automation of complex workflows

Most of my projects explore practical applications of AI systems, including document retrieval pipelines, agent-driven workflows, and intelligent decision-support tools.
